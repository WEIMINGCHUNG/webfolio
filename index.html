Interaction Design Researcher

I am is a human–computer interaction researcher and worked with Prof. Rung-Huei Liang in the Spatial Media Research Group. I received my Ph.D. in 2021 from the Department of Design at National Taiwan University of Science and Technology, specializing in exploring and creating novel designerly tools for emerging technologies.
I previously worked as a research assistant at National Taiwan University's IoX Center, where I supported the Design Fiction Project from ideation to design practice and research, and collaborated with research engineers in computer science and electrical engineering.
My research interests include creativity support, designerly tools, intermediate-level knowledge, and design fiction.
